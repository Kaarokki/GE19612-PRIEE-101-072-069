YIN YOGA

Focus : Long-held, passive poses targeting deep connective tissues.

Benefits :Improves flexibility, joint mobility, and stress relief.

Intensity : Low,deeply relaxing and meditative.

VINYASA YOGA

Focus :Flowing sequences of poses coordinated with breath.

Benefits :Builds strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular health.

Intensity :Varies,can range from moderate to high.

POWER YOGA

Focus :Vigorous, fitness-based approach to Vinyasa yoga.

Benefits :Increases strength, endurance, and flexibility.

Intensity :High; ideal for those wanting a workout.

KUNDAKINI YOGA

Focus :Awakening the energy at the base of the spine through breath, movement, and chanting.

Benefits : Enhances mental clarity, emotional stability, and physical vitality.

Intensity :Varies; integrates physical and spiritual practices.

HATHA YOGA

Focus :Fundamental poses, breathing techniques, and meditation.

Benefits :Improves flexibility, balance, and relaxation.

Intensity :Moderate; suitable for beginners.

RESTORATIVE YOGA

Focus :Gentle poses supported by props to promote deep relaxation.

Benefits :Reduces stress, aids recovery, and promotes overall well-being.

Intensity :Very low; suitable for all fitness levels.
