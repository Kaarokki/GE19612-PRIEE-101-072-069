CARDIOVASCULAR WORKOUT

Focus: Improving heart rate and endurance through rhythmic exercises.

Benefits: Boosts cardiovascular health, burns calories, and increases stamina.

Intensity: Moderate to high, depending on exercise intensity and duration.

HIGH-INTENSITY INTERVAL TRAINING (HIIT)

Focus: Alternating between intense bursts of activity and short periods of rest or lower-intensity exercises.

Benefits: Efficient calorie burning, improves cardiovascular fitness, and enhances metabolism.

Intensity: High intensity, challenging cardiovascular and muscular endurance.

PILATES

Focus: Strengthens core muscles, improves posture, and enhances flexibility through controlled movements.

Benefits: Develops core strength, increases muscle tone, and promotes mind-body connection.

Intensity: Moderate, focuses on controlled movements and proper form.

CROSSFIT

Focus: Varied functional movements performed at high intensity.

Benefits: Enhances strength, agility, and endurance across different fitness domains.

Intensity: High intensity, combines weightlifting, cardio, and gymnastics elements.

ZUMBA

Focus: Dance-based fitness program combining Latin and international music with dance movements.

Benefits: Fun aerobic workout, improves cardiovascular health, coordination, and mood.

Intensity: Moderate to high, depending on the pace and intensity of dance movements.
