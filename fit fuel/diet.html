MEDITERRANEAN DIET


Overview:
Focus: Emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, olive oil, nuts, and seafood.
Benefits: Promotes heart health, weight management, and overall well-being.
Intensity: Moderate; focuses on balanced nutrition and portion control.

Sample Schedule:

Monday to Friday:

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with honey and mixed berries.
Lunch: Quinoa salad with cucumbers, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese.
Dinner: Grilled chicken breast with roasted vegetables and whole grain couscous.

Saturday:

Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and poached eggs.
Lunch: Mediterranean-style wrap with hummus, grilled vegetables, and feta cheese.
Dinner: Baked cod with Mediterranean salsa (tomatoes, olives, capers, herbs) and quinoa.

Sunday:

Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, almond milk, and chia seeds.
Lunch: Grilled lamb chops with Greek salad (tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, feta).
Dinner: Whole wheat pasta with marinara sauce, spinach, and grilled shrimp.


KETO DIET

Overview: The Keto diet focuses on high-fat, low-carb intake to induce ketosis, a metabolic state that burns fat for fuel.
Benefits: Promotes weight loss, controls blood sugar levels, and enhances mental clarity.
Intensity: High; restricts carbs to around 20-50 grams per day.

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs cooked in butter with avocado slices.

Dinner: Grilled shrimp skewers with a side salad drizzled with olive oil.

Sunday:

Breakfast: Keto-friendly smoothie with almond milk, spinach, avocado, and protein powder.
Lunch: Pork chops cooked in coconut oil with cauliflower rice.
Dinner: Zucchini noodles with pesto sauce and grilled chicken breast.

VEGETARIAN DIET

Overview: The Vegetarian diet emphasizes plant-based foods such as fruits, vegetables, grains, legumes, nuts, and seeds, excluding meat and fish.
Benefits: Promotes heart health, weight management, and environmental sustainability.
Intensity: Moderate; focuses on balanced nutrition and variety in plant-based sources.

Sample Schedule:

Monday to Friday:

Breakfast: Oatmeal topped with mixed berries and almond slices.
Lunch: Chickpea and vegetable stir-fry with brown rice.
Dinner: Lentil soup with whole grain bread and a side salad.

Saturday:

Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with spinach, banana, almond milk, and chia seeds.
Lunch: Quinoa salad with roasted vegetables, avocado, and a tahini dressing.
Dinner: Stuffed bell peppers with quinoa, black beans, corn, and salsa.

Sunday:

Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with honey, granola, and fresh fruits.
Lunch: Pasta primavera with whole wheat pasta, mixed vegetables, and marinara sauce.
Dinner: Baked tofu with steamed broccoli and quinoa pilaf.

PALEO DIET

Overview: The Paleo diet focuses on foods that would have been available during the Paleolithic era, including lean meats, fish, fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds.
Benefits: Promotes weight loss, improved digestion, and overall health.
Intensity: Moderate to high; emphasizes whole foods and avoids processed items.

Sample Schedule:

Monday to Friday:

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and avocado.
Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, tomatoes, and olive oil dressing.
Dinner: Baked salmon with roasted vegetables (sweet potatoes, broccoli, and carrots).

Saturday:

Breakfast: Paleo pancakes made with almond flour, topped with berries and maple syrup.
Lunch: Turkey lettuce wraps with avocado, tomato, and salsa.
Dinner: Grass-fed beef burgers wrapped in lettuce, served with sweet potato fries.

Sunday:

Breakfast: Smoothie with coconut milk, banana, berries, and chia seeds.
Lunch: Grilled shrimp with zucchini noodles and pesto sauce.
Dinner: Roast chicken with garlic and herbs, served with steamed asparagus.

DASH DIET

Overview: The DASH diet focuses on lowering sodium intake and emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and low-fat dairy.
Benefits: Reduces blood pressure, improves heart health, and supports weight loss.
Intensity: Moderate; promotes balanced nutrition and portion control.

Sample Schedule:

Monday to Friday:

Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and low-fat milk.
Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, tomatoes, and olive oil vinaigrette.
Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and almonds.
Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed vegetables.

Saturday:

Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and poached eggs.
Lunch: Turkey and vegetable stir-fry with brown rice.
Snack: Apple slices with peanut butter.
Dinner: Lean beef tacos with whole wheat tortillas, salsa, and guacamole.

Sunday:

Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, low-fat yogurt, and flaxseeds.
Lunch: Lentil soup with whole grain roll and side salad.
Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus.
Dinner: Grilled shrimp skewers with quinoa pilaf and roasted vegetables.
